Quick Start

  • Prompt Builder: Add subjects, styles, and lighting keywords. Toggle Magic Enhance to auto-extend prompts.
  • Image to Prompt: Upload JPG, PNG, or WebP images up to 10 MB. We validate files before processing.
  • Batch Generator: Enter a base idea and let the generator craft unique variations for testing.

Data & Privacy

Uploads are validated on a secured serverless function and never written to disk. Prompts stay in your browser unless you choose to share them.
