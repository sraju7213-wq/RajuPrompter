Overview

Raju Prompt Suite is designed with privacy-first principles. We only process the minimum amount of data needed to generate prompts and never sell, rent, or trade your information.

What We Collect

  • Prompt text: Stored locally in your browser for history and saved prompts. Content never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • Usage metrics: We may collect anonymous, aggregated analytics (e.g., feature usage counts) to improve the experience. These metrics contain no personal data.

Your Choices

  • Clear your prompt history and saved prompts at any time from the app interface.
  • Disable analytics by using a browser or network-level tracker blocker—core features continue to work.
  • Request deletion of any optional data you shared with us by contacting hello@rajuprompter.app.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy or need additional assurances, email hello@rajuprompter.app. We respond to every request within five business days.
